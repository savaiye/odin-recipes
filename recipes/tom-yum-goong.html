Tom Yum Goong

Tom Yum Goong: A Spicy, Zesty Soup for the Adventurous Palate!

Tom Yum Goong is a spicy and sour shrimp soup that reflects Thailand's flavor profile. With roots in central Thailand, this dish has been popular for centuries, often enjoyed for its aromatic herbs and immune-boosting properties.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 stalk lemongrass (cut into 2-inch pieces)
  • 3-4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1-inch piece galangal (sliced)
  • 1-2 bird's eye chilies (crushed)
  • 200g shrimp (peeled)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil water or broth with lemongrass, lime leaves, galangal, and chilies for 5 minutes.
  2. Add shrimp and mushrooms, simmer until shrimp are cooked.
  3. Season with fish sauce and lime juice to taste.
  4. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
